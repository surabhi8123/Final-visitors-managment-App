THORSIGNIA LOGO PLACEHOLDER

To add your ThorSignia logo:

1. Create a PNG image with your ThorSignia logo
2. Save it as "logo.png" in this folder
3. The logo should include:
   - Triangular graphic elements (lime green, teal, dark gray)
   - "Thor Signia" text (lime green "Thor", teal "Signia")
   - "Committed Towards Progress" tagline (dark gray)
   - White background
   - Recommended size: 240x160px

For now, the app displays "VS" initials in a rectangular container.
